A little about me

Teacher, trainer, thinker — and a passionate chess player

I'm Dr Andrew Chew, founder of Impactus Learning and an AI trainer at NTUC LearningHub and NTU. By day I help working professionals navigate the world of generative AI and digital marketing. By night (and many a weekend), you'll find me deep in thought over a chessboard — plotting, scheming, and occasionally sacrificing my queen with a big grin.

Chess, to me, is more than a game. It's a mirror of life: every move has consequences, patience is rewarded, and the best plan is always the one you're flexible enough to abandon. Also — it keeps the brain young!

The hobby

Why chess captured my heart ♟

Chess found me at a time when I needed a mental workout that had nothing to do with slides or lesson plans. There's something incredibly calming about a game where the whole universe shrinks to 64 squares — and yet those 64 squares contain more possible games than there are atoms in the observable universe.

I love how chess teaches you to think ahead — skills that translate beautifully into teaching, business strategy, and even parenting!

  • Favourite piece: the Knight — unpredictable, just like me
  • Current obsession: the Sicilian Defence
  • Chess philosophy: attack with joy, defend with patience
  • Plays on: Lichess and over-the-board with family
